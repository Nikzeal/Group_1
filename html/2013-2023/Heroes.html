/ Heroes died, but will always be remembered /

2023 was a wonderful year for technology. But, while anything less than immortality is, ultimately, unaccettable, humanity still is mortal. Thus, some heroes for the history of technology, died this year. We will always remember them, and, hopefully, they will be remembered.

  • Gordon Moore, the inventor of the Moore's law, passed away on march.
  • Kevin Mitnick, passed away in July. He invented the IP Spoofing technique.
  • Molly Holszchlang passed away in September. She was a famous writer for the Open Web, and wrote or co-wrote many books on web design, and css.
