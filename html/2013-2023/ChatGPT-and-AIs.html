/ ChatGPT /

2022 was a big year for AIs. This is due to many reasons, the release in america just on December of 2021 of Tesla D1's chip, for starters. But, the biggest change was made by OpenAI. Finally, after years and work, their AI... exploded! Not literally, of course. In popularity. 2022 was the biggest year yet for Artificial Intelligences. ChatGPT's popularity increased, exploded- as said- and the world finally became aware- and obsessed- with computers. Let us check closer on AIs during this year:

  • From 2017, the AI adoption has increased. It's twice as higher.
  • AIs are becoming more and more capable, and are used more and more for organizations over the past few years
  • AIs are used more and more in products and business models as well
  • AIs are used most popularly for service operation platforms
  • AIs are becoming cheaper and cheaper, maybe following Moore's law?
  • There has been no substantial increase in organizations' reported mitigation of AI-related risks
  • And of course, the more people are becoming aware of AIs, the more some people start to panick about it...


/ Thus, we talk about AIs and society /

As ChatGPT in particular popularity's increased, AIs became more common for people. Used more and more for daily tasks, and for newer things, society took it... with surprisingly open mind. Until... Jason Allen entered the Theatre d'Opera Spatial, into the Colorado State Fair. He... won. His piece won. And it was AI made! It was such an incredible feat! And AI generated images, boomed. More and more spread across multiple sites, Pinterest, Pixiv, and so on. And with the spread of AIs image generators, people started hating on these tools. Afraid they would "steal their job", people started criticizing heavily these tools. After AI image generators, AI creative writing tools followed due. With the boom of criticism, many and more discussions were born about AIs ethical uses, and wether or not it should be developed... or stopped.
