/ Google began drone-based delivery via Google Wing in select areas /



Google Affiliate Starts Using Drones to Deliver Packages in Virginia TestA revolutionary new delivery service has taken flight in Christiansburg, Virginia.

Wing partnered with Walgreens, FedEx, and a local gift shop called Sugar Magnolia to make the deliveries. Customers can order from a list of more than 100 items from Walgreens and have them delivered to their doors by drone.
The drones can fly up to 4 miles (6.5 kilometers) from Wing's distribution facility in Christiansburg. They are capable of flying a 12-mile (19-kilometer) round trip, and Wing expects to widen its delivery radius in the future.

Wing has already launched tests in Canberra and Logan City, Australia, and Helsinki, Finland. But Friday's flights mark its first live commercial deliveries in the United States




/ Folding phones - thats old..? /

The Samsung Galaxy Fold is a remarkable smartphone that revolutionized the mobile phone industry with its innovative foldable design. Unveiled in 2019, it was the first of its kind to feature a large, flexible display that folds in half, transforming from a compact phone into a tablet-sized device. This groundbreaking design offered users a versatile and immersive experience, allowing them to seamlessly switch between tasks and enjoy content on a larger screen.
The Galaxy Fold's innovative technology extends beyond its foldable display. It boasts a powerful Snapdragon 855 processor, ensuring smooth performance for even the most demanding tasks. Its six-camera system, consisting of three rear cameras, two front cameras, and one cover camera, captures stunning photos and videos in various lighting conditions. Additionally, its 4380mAh battery provides ample power to keep users connected throughout the day.



/ Starlink: The Future of High-Speed Internet /



Starlink is a satellite internet constellation developed by SpaceX, the aerospace company founded by Elon Musk. It is designed to provide high-speed internet access to people living in rural and underserved areas. The constellation is currently in beta testing, but it is already providing internet access to thousands of people around the world.

Starlink is unique in that it uses a network of low-Earth orbit (LEO) satellites. These satellites orbit Earth at a much lower altitude than traditional geostationary satellites, which means that they can provide lower latency internet. Latency is the time it takes for data to travel from one point to another, and it is important for applications that require real-time communication, such as online gaming and video conferencing.

Starlink has the potential to revolutionize the way we access the internet. It can provide high-speed internet access to people who currently have no other options, and it can help to bridge the digital divide between urban and rural areas. Starlink is still in its early stages of development, but it has the potential to change the world for the better.
