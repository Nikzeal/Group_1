With the release of these two new headsets, Oculus is making it easier than ever to get into VR gaming. The Rift S is a great option for PC gamers who want a high-quality VR experience, while the standalone headset is perfect for casual gamers who want a more mobile experience.

/ Gaming on pc will improve - AMD Ryzen 9 ?? /


In 2019, AMD released two high-performance desktop processors, the Ryzen 9 3900X and Ryzen 9 3950X, that revolutionized the gaming and content creation landscape. These powerful chips offered significant performance improvements over previous generations, making it easier than ever to enjoy demanding games and create stunning content.
The Ryzen 9 3900X, a 12-core, 24-thread processor, quickly became a favorite among gamers due to its high performance and affordable price. It could handle even the most intense multiplayer battles and immersive open-world environments with ease.
For content creators, the Ryzen 9 3950X was the ultimate choice. Its 16 cores and 32 threads provided the processing power needed to tackle even the most demanding creative tasks, such as rendering high-resolution videos, editing complex photo projects, and creating intricate 3D animations.
AMD's Ryzen 9 processors ushered in a new era of gaming and content creation, making it easier than ever to enjoy the most demanding experiences. With their high performance, affordable pricing, and power efficiency, the Ryzen 9 3900X and Ryzen 9 3950X quickly became the go-to choices for discerning gamers, content creators, and tech enthusiasts worldwide.



/ 5G - get ready, we are connecting /




5G's impact extends far beyond just speed. Its ultra-low latency, the time it takes for a signal to travel from one point to another, will open up new frontiers in real-time applications. Imagine self-driving cars communicating with each other and with infrastructure in real time, or surgeons performing operations remotely with the precision and dexterity of being physically present.

5G's massive capacity will enable the connection of billions of devices, paving the way for the Internet of Things (IoT) to truly take off. Smart homes, connected cities, and industrial automation will all be powered by 5G, creating a world where everything is connected and data is exchanged seamlessly.

5G is not just a new technology; it is a transformative force that will reshape our world. Its impact will be felt in every aspect of our lives, from the way we communicate and work to the way we consume entertainment and interact with our environment.
